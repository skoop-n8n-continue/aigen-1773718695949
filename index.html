Skoop Signage Tutorial

Learn how to create a new playlist and add media items to your digital signage display.

1 Accessing the Platform

Navigate to https://cloud.skoopsignage.com/ and enter your credentials to log in.

2 Navigate to Playlists

Once logged in, navigate to the Playlists section using the sidebar menu.

3 Create a New Playlist

Click the Create Playlist button in the top right corner of the screen.

4 Name and Save

Enter a descriptive name for your playlist in the Playlist Name field, then click Save.

5 Open the Playlist

Find your new playlist in the list and click on it to open the editor.

6 Add Media

In the right-hand sidebar under Content, click on Media.

7 Select and Confirm

Select the desired media item from your library and click Add or Confirm to include it in your playlist.

8 Final Result

Your media item is now added to the playlist! You can continue adding more content or publish your changes.
